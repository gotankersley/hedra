Rules:

Objective

Each player tries to form a path connecting their home base(s), before the other players in order to win. A player may also win by removing all of their opponents' pieces.

Paths

A path is formed by crossing over an octagonal tile. No path is formed when just moving from adjacent quad to another.

Jumping

Opponent pieces may be removed by 'jumping' over them in a manner similar to jumping in Checkers.

Optional Rules:

Hex Path (Spheres) Allow hexagons to be used as part of the path - however, instead of the usual way of creating a path, (which doesn't work due to the shape of hexagons). The hex is added to the path by having at least 2 pieces on adjacent quads
Minor Radius Win (Torus) An additional way for players to win by connecting a path around the minor radius of the torus instead of to the other home base
Wormholes These allow transport from one wormhole to another by crossing over the wormhole like creating a path.
Notes:
  1. It can be blocked on the sending side if the tile being crossed is not empty.
  2. If the destination is not empty, then it will remove any pieces on the destination, regardless of side.
